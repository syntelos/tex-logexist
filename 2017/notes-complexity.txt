In the complexity of the mind-body identity, unity, exists character as a genetic trait and the space of imagination as a temporal feature.  Within the temporal complexity of practice (daily life) ranges the peace and violence of scholarship and absurdity.  


The imagination engages the world at a distance that varies with well being and the disciplines of self awareness.  In the imagination we process tangible experience and intangible communication into the objects of abstraction that are practical or immediate understanding and motivation and inhibition.  The disciplines of self awareness permit the individual to handle the objects of imagination as one handles the objects of physicality.  With caution or recklessness according to one’s discipline of self awareness.
