It is to realize oneself in the world, to live life on a path of personal discovery and actualization.  The path exists in the Metaphysical Realm of self and world.  The spiritual self and spiritual others in the material context of body and planet and economics formal and informal (official and unofficial).  


We each elect a degree of the challenge of social constraints.  At one end of the spectrum the nomad who rejects society as constraining of the human experience, and at the other the socialite who accepts society as elevating the human experience.  The intersection of social constraint against breadth of experience remains variable within this linear order, producing a surface.
